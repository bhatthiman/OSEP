## Why AGPL for OSEP

OSEP is intended to remain an open engineering platform.

The AGPL license ensures that:

* Modifications remain open-source
* Network/server deployments also share source changes
* Engineering improvements benefit the broader ecosystem
* Open collaboration remains protected
* Browser-based deployments cannot silently become closed forks

This aligns with the long-term vision of OSEP as an open engineering platform for static equipment modeling, fabrication-aware workflows, and engineering collaboration.
